Styles

Digital services for government staff users in the NHSBSA (new services and existing digital services that are being redeveloped) should use these styles.

In the NHSBSA this includes:

  • caseworking systems
  • microsites
  • intranets

Why we created these styles

This guidance applies to digital services, and does not affect or change any of the NHSBSA's existing brand advice for print and offline applications.
